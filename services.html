Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by project type. A brand identity takes 1–3 weeks. A website takes 2–4 weeks. Custom software typically runs 4–12 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline during your consultation.

Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

Both. We work with early-stage startups who need to launch with conviction, and established businesses who need to scale or rebrand. The common thread is ambition — not company size.

What's your payment structure?

We typically work on a 50% upfront, 50% on delivery model for smaller projects. Larger engagements are broken into milestone-based payments aligned to project phases.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. We offer retainer-based ongoing support and maintenance packages, as well as ad-hoc support. We prefer long-term relationships — our best client relationships span years, not weeks.

Can you handle multiple services at once?

Absolutely. Many of our best engagements involve building a brand identity, website, and automation systems simultaneously. Working with one studio for everything means perfect cohesion.
